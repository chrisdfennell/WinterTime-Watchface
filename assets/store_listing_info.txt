Snowfall — Winter Alpine Watch Face

(Alternates: "Snowfall — Winter Digital Watch Face" /
 "Snowfall: Aurora & Snow Watch Face")


------------------------------------------------------------------------
FIELD: Description (Max 4000 characters)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Snowfall is a premium digital watch face with a crisp, serene winter alpine aesthetic — a living winter sky, aurora over a starfield, drifting clouds, falling snow, and a snow-laden pine, all procedurally rendered. Large outlined time and clean complications keep you connected all winter.

FEATURES & DETAILS
• LIVING WINTER SKY: A procedural backdrop that shifts color by time of day — deep indigo night, cold rose dawn, pale midday blue, cold-peach sunset, twilight purple.
• AURORA & STARS: Wavy green/cyan/violet aurora ribbons drift above a field of stars through the long winter nights.
• ARCING SUN & MOON: A pale winter sun and silver crescent moon arc across day and night, driven by the REAL sunrise/sunset for your location and date — sky, day/night, and aurora all follow the true sun times.
• PROCEDURAL ANIMATIONS: Drifting clouds, rolling snow-drift layers, falling snow, and a swaying snow-laden pine silhouette.
• FESTIVE MODE: Flip one switch to deck the halls — Santa's sleigh and reindeer (Rudolph and all) sweep the sky every couple of minutes, the pine lights up with a sparkling star topper and blinking multicolor strings, and a Star of Bethlehem shines at night. Toggle off for the pure alpine look.
• WINTER CRITTERS: Occasional visitors cross the scene — one at a time, every ~40 seconds. By day: a red cardinal, a snowshoe hare, a snow-pouncing red fox, a pecking chickadee. By night: a snowy owl, an arctic fox, a howling grey wolf, and a steaming-breath stag. One switch turns them on or off.
• ROTATING SECONDS: A six-armed snowflake second indicator orbiting the perimeter.
• CONFIGURABLE COMPLICATIONS: Set the bottom-left and bottom-right to Heart Rate (live BPM), Body Battery, Device Battery (live fill bar), Steps, Calories, or Off, each with a matching icon. Heart rate samples at most once every ~10 seconds to spare the battery. A frosted-ice steps progress bar with a numeric count sits in the center.
• HIGH-CONTRAST OUTLINES: All texts carry a crisp black outline for readability against the dynamic backdrops.

BUILT FOR YOUR DISPLAY
• AMOLED (Fenix 8, Forerunner 965/970, Venu, epix, Instinct 3 AMOLED, and more): smooth gradient fills, specular highlights, and active background illumination.
• MIP / Solar (Fenix 7, Fenix 8 Solar, Forerunner 255/955, Instinct, and more): high-contrast flat fills tuned for sunlight readability.
Fonts are baked for every resolution, so it looks right from 166px Instinct panels up to 454px flagships.

ALWAYS-ON FRIENDLY
On AMOLED, the always-on display switches to a burn-in-safe variant — dim grey time/date, thin outline battery metrics and steps progress, no fills or animation, and lit pixels shifted each minute. MIP panels always show the full face.

SETTINGS
• Show Date — toggle the date line.
• Festive Mode — toggle Santa's sleigh flyover and the sparkling lit Christmas tree.
• Winter Critters — toggle the occasional crossing visitors (fox, hare, cardinal, chickadee, owl, arctic fox, wolf, stag).
• Step Goal Override — steps for a full progress bar; 0 uses the watch's own step goal.
• Bottom-Left Complication — Off / Heart Rate / Body Battery / Device Battery / Steps / Calories.
• Bottom-Right Complication — same options.

PRIVACY
Snowfall uses only the SensorHistory permission (to read Body Battery). Heart rate and last-known location are read on-device to show your BPM and compute local sunrise/sunset; no data is collected or transmitted off your watch.

SUPPORTED DEVICES
Every Connect IQ 4.0+ round watch, including: Forerunner 165, 255/255S, 265/265S, 570, 955, 965, 970; fenix 7 / 7S / 7X (incl. Pro), fenix 8 (AMOLED & Solar), fenix E; epix 2 / epix Pro (42/47/51mm); enduro 3; Venu 2 / 2S / 2 Plus, Venu 3 / 3S, Venu 4; Vivoactive 5 / 6; Instinct 3 (AMOLED & Solar), Instinct E, Instinct Crossover; Approach S50 / S70; Descent G2 / Mk3; D2 Air X10 / Mach 1 / Mach 2; MARQ 2 / Aviator. (Square Venu Sq 2 and Venu X1 are not supported.)

Equip Snowfall, and take a moment in the snow.
