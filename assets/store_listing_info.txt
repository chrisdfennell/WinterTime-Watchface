Snowfall — Winter Alpine Watch Face

(Alternates: "Snowfall — Winter Digital Watch Face" /
 "Snowfall: Aurora & Snow Watch Face")


------------------------------------------------------------------------
FIELD: Description (Max 4000 characters)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Snowfall is a premium, high-fidelity digital watch face featuring a crisp, serene winter alpine aesthetic. Experience a living winter sky, aurora ribbons over a starfield, drifting clouds, rolling snow drifts, falling snow, and a snow-laden pine silhouette, all procedurally rendered. Large, outlined digital time and clean complications keep you connected and styled for winter.

FEATURES & DETAILS
• LIVING WINTER SKY: A procedural backdrop that shifts colors dynamically by time of day (deep indigo night, cold rose dawn, pale midday blue, cold-peach sunset, and twilight purple).
• AURORA & STARS: Wavy green/cyan/violet aurora borealis ribbons drift above a field of stars through the long winter nights.
• ARCING SUN & MOON: Day/night orbital progression of a pale winter sun (with faint rotating rays and a cold halo) and a silver crescent moon, driven by the REAL sunrise/sunset for your location and date — the sky, day/night, and aurora all follow the true sun times.
• PROCEDURAL ANIMATIONS: Drifting clouds, rolling snow-drift layers, falling snow, and a swaying snow-laden pine silhouette.
• FESTIVE MODE: Flip one switch to deck the halls — Santa's sleigh and reindeer (Rudolph's nose and all) sweep across the sky every couple of minutes, the pine tree lights up with a sparkling star topper and strings of blinking multicolor lights, and a radiant Star of Bethlehem shines in the night sky. Toggle it all off for the pure alpine look.
• ROTATING SECONDS: A six-armed snowflake second indicator floating and orbiting the perimeter.
• CONFIGURABLE COMPLICATIONS: Choose what the bottom-left and bottom-right complications show — Heart Rate (live BPM), Body Battery, Device Battery (with a live fill bar), Steps, Calories, or Off — each with a matching icon (heart, bolt, battery, boot, flame). Heart rate is sampled at most once every ~10 seconds to spare the battery. A frosted-ice steps progress bar with a numeric count sits in the center.
• HIGH-CONTRAST OUTLINES: All texts (clock, date, weather, and battery metrics) feature a crisp black outline for absolute readability against the dynamic visual backdrops.

BUILT FOR YOUR DISPLAY
Snowfall adapts to your panel:
• AMOLED (Fenix 8 / tactix 8 / Fenix 8 Pro, Forerunner 965/970, Venu, epix, Instinct 3 AMOLED, and more): smooth gradient resource fills, specular highlights, and active background illumination.
• MIP / Solar (Fenix 7, Fenix 8 Solar, Forerunner 255/955, Instinct, and more): high-contrast flat fills and optimized backdrops tuned for maximum sunlight readability.
Everything is laid out relative to your screen, with correctly-sized fonts baked for every resolution, so it looks right on every supported case size — from 166px Instinct panels up to 454px flagships.

ALWAYS-ON FRIENDLY
On AMOLED, the always-on display switches to a burn-in-safe variant — dim grey time/date, thin outline representations of the battery metrics, steps progress outline, and no visual fills or background animations. All lit pixels are shifted a few pixels each minute. MIP panels always show the full face.

SETTINGS
• Show Date — toggle the date line.
• Festive Mode — toggle Santa's sleigh flyover and the sparkling lit Christmas tree.
• Step Goal Override — steps for a full progress bar; 0 uses the watch's own step goal.
• Bottom-Left Complication — Off / Heart Rate / Body Battery / Device Battery / Steps / Calories.
• Bottom-Right Complication — same options.

PRIVACY
Snowfall uses only the SensorHistory permission (to read Body Battery). It reads heart rate and last-known location on-device to show your BPM and compute local sunrise/sunset; it does not collect or transmit any data off your watch.

SUPPORTED DEVICES
Every Connect IQ 4.0+ round watch, including: Forerunner 165, 255/255S, 265/265S, 570, 955, 965, 970; fenix 7 / 7S / 7X (incl. Pro), fenix 8 (AMOLED & Solar), fenix E; epix 2 / epix Pro (42/47/51mm); enduro 3; Venu 2 / 2S / 2 Plus, Venu 3 / 3S, Venu 4; Vivoactive 5 / 6; Instinct 3 (AMOLED & Solar), Instinct E, Instinct Crossover; Approach S50 / S70; Descent G2 / Mk3; D2 Air X10 / Mach 1 / Mach 2; MARQ 2 / Aviator. (Square/rectangular Venu Sq 2 and Venu X1 are not supported — the circular layout is built for round screens.)

Equip Snowfall, and take a moment in the snow.
